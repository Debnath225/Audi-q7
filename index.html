Audi Q7

The Audi Q7 is a luxury SUV that combines performance, technology, and comfort. It features a spacious interior, advanced safety features, and a powerful engine.

View Details
  • Engine: 3.0L V6 Turbocharged
  • Horsepower: 335 hp
  • Torque: 369 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • All-Wheel Drive (Quattro)
  • Seating Capacity: 7 passengers
  • Infotainment: MMI Touch Response system with dual touchscreens
  • Safety: Audi pre sense, adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning
  • Fuel Economy: 19 mpg city / 23 mpg highway
  • Price: Starting at $54,900

About Audi

Audi is a German automobile manufacturer that designs, engineers, produces, markets and distributes luxury vehicles. Audi is a member of the Volkswagen Group and has its roots at Ingolstadt, Bavaria, Germany. Audi-branded vehicles are produced in nine production facilities worldwide.
